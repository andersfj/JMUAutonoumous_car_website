Feb 20, 2018
As the team’s prepared for their first week of concrete progress, each team had questions, concerns, and ideas that they hoped would put the class on the right track.

This week was especially dedicated to getting all of the teams on board for each communication tool that would be used during the semester.

The class reviewed each other’s progress presentations. The main concerns was WHEN was all this WHAT going to happen, we needed a timeline.

Many team’s had begun to toy with the programs and products they wanted to use and it felt like everyone was motivated about the challenge. It was time to get realistic and make a plan.
